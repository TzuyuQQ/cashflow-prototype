Cash Flow Management Tool — Prototype Review 2 / 6

The Problem

What PMO Struggles With Today

📊
Data is Scattered Everywhere
Each project has its own Excel file. PMO has to manually combine them to see the full picture — which is slow and error-prone.
⚠️
Problems Are Found Too Late
Overdue invoices and cash shortfalls are only noticed after they happen. There is no early warning when something is going wrong.
👤
No Way to Track Each PM's Performance
PMO cannot quickly check which Project Manager has late payments, unpaid invoices, or cash flow issues across their projects.
🔮
No Forward-Looking Cash View
Without a 12-month forecast, Finance and PMO cannot plan ahead for cash gaps or decide when to speed up collections or delay payments.
Cash Flow Management Tool — Prototype Review 3 / 6

The Solution

What This Tool Does

✓
One place for all project cash data — see the full portfolio at a glance: net cash, overdue AR, upcoming payments, and at-risk projects.
✓
Filter by Project Manager on every page — PMO can instantly switch to any PM's view to check their cash situation.
✓
AR tracking with DSO and aging — see which clients owe money, how long it has been outstanding, and collection speed.
✓
12-month cash forecast — see expected cash in and out each month, with a drill-down into each project.
✓
Full transaction log — every cash entry is searchable by project, type, status, and date.
